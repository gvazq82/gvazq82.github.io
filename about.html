About Me

I was born in one of the biggest cities in the world, Mexico City , more than 20 million people sharing 1,485 km2 of space; traffic and pollution drives you crazy some times, but the food, amazing weather and extensive cultural options makes you to consider moving away.

Graduated in 2004 from UPIICSA, IPN obtained a Bachelor degree in Computer's Science. I feel proud of alma mater, hope some day I come back and start imparting classes to new generations.

I started working since I was 15 years old, some part time jobs, worked as valet parking, McDonald's employee and at a small groceries store in the neighborhood, always working hard, always working good.

It was until 2005 when I got an internship as Software Engineer at E-Siglo, working as a Java consultant for some important banks in Mexico City.

In 2007 I found a very good opportunity working as Software Engineer for Softtek Information Services in Aguascalientes, Mexico , located at 490 km at north of Mexico City, famous for the San Marcos Fair during April and May.
